OUR CONCEPTS
How do we conserve our cultural heritage?

Inspiration

Traditional handicraft as a beautiful artwork made completely by hand and simple tool has no story tells the products. Our products tell you a story behind history and local wisdom available in English and Javanese script on our high quality cover wrapped by Jatayu bird design as a guard of precious one in Wayang Ramayana philosophy. We inspired to make an educational artwork.

Composition

Wayang made from wood-based materials of klepu and finest quality of teak are applied in high-quality fretwork. Box made from wood-based materials of MDF are covered with high-quality art paper. Our products made from residual wood of furniture production are environmentally friendly and recyclable. We compose an eco-friendly handicraft product.
